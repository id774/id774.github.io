About Me

Software engineer, raised in Seijo, Setagaya and currently residing near the heart of Tokyo.

I have a sustained interest in the structures that govern people, money, and society.

My work has consistently focused on the economic systems behind finance and securities, often involving analytical approaches grounded in statistical mathematics and the social sciences.

Previously, I was involved in system development for the securities industry and conducted research on innovative investment strategies using machine learning.

I spent a substantial period enjoying an unrestricted lifestyle amidst the eclectic corners of downtown Tokyo, free from the constraints of conventional schedules and organizational structures.

I now lead a data engineering team focused on building and maintaining data infrastructure for asset management analytics.

Please refer to the Links for my activities on social media and to the Apps for the products I've created.
